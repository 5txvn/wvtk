How Word Analogies Work

Vector Representation

Each word is converted to its corresponding vector in high-dimensional space.

Vector Arithmetic

We perform the operation: result = word2 - word1 + word3 to find the analogy vector.

Similarity Search

We find the words most similar to the resulting analogy vector.
